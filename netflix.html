Netflix Clone

github | live link

Built with: JSX, CSS, REACT.JS

Fancy watching a movie? Sign Up for the best movie streaming platform out there. Once Signed use the search bar to find your desired movie or just browse until you find something that is for your taste.

A big Netflix Clone Project. Full of the most recent and modern React.js features. Here you can find everything from Costum Hooks, The Context API, Styled Components, Compound Components and much more. Within the app there are 4 different pages: sign in, sign up, browse and the home page. Some of the pages are using protected routes with auth listeners since there is a sign in functionality. Firebase firestore is handling all the data. A fantastic and very fun project to build.
